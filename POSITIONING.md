# ModCom positioning — AI workflow (behaviour-first)

## Core message

> **Your AI does the analysis. ModCom keeps the record.**

## Supporting lines

- Compliance that starts where your thinking starts.
- Import your AI-generated incident in under 60 seconds.
- We noticed how founders actually work now. We removed the friction.

## Do say

- Destination for work already happening in AI chats
- Structured import, human review, audit-ready provenance
- Format once, paste into ModCom

## Don’t say

- “AI-powered compliance platform”
- “Auto-filed by AI” / “Trusted AI output”
- Claims that AI output is audit-ready without human sign-off
- Hard dependency on a single AI vendor

## Product truth

We are not building the AI. We are building the **structure and destination** so compliance work lands cleanly, with mandatory human review before anything is live.
